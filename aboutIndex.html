Welcome to ShipDelay!

a tool to calculate your inventory needs

ShipDelay is for any growing business that needs help re-ordering their inventory. This app is especially helpful for business that don't have much warehouse/storage space and aren't able to store a lot of inventory. This app is designed to replace inventory once it reaches near zero with a buffer of 3 days to make up for any potential delays.
